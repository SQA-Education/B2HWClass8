1. Reading Comprehension

Dealing with Stress: Sarah and Ahmed

Sarah has been so stressed lately and is looking for ways to cope. Her colleague Ahmed has recently made some positive changes. He has started meditating every morning, which has made him feel relaxed and comfortable. He has also learned to breathe deeply and unwind after stressful days.

Ahmed encourages Sarah to try mindfulness. Sarah has felt frustrated with work and thinks mindfulness could help. In another conversation, a therapist asks a patient about recent emotions. The patient has felt devastated and confused after a breakup, but has started to journal and take breaks when feeling overwhelmed. These self-care practices have helped them feel a bit more calm and comfortable.

Self-care is any action you take to maintain your physical and mental health. It can include meditating, journaling, talking it out with a friend, or simply letting go of negative emotions. The key is to find what works for you.
